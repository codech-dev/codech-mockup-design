# Differentiation Axes Reference

When creating multiple design directions, use this reference to ensure each version is genuinely different — not just a reskin. Each direction should differ on at least 5 of these 7 axes.

## The 7 axes

### 1. Layout primitive
The fundamental unit of the page.
- **Card grid** — uniform rectangular tiles (the most common default)
- **Horizontal scroll rows** — Netflix/Spotify-style category channels
- **Bento grid** — asymmetric mixed-size tiles (Apple marketing-style)
- **List rows** — data-table-style dense rows (Linear/Notion backlog)
- **Magazine spread** — asymmetric editorial columns
- **Single-screen sequential** — one screen at a time, scroll to advance
- **Canvas / spatial** — zoomable 2D surface
- **Split-pane** — sidebar + main content (app layout)

### 2. Typography model
The font pairing strategy.
- **Serif display + sans body** — editorial/magazine feel (Playfair + Inter)
- **Contemporary serif only** — Fraunces, Instrument Serif, Literata
- **Single geometric sans** — Geist, Satoshi, General Sans, Inter
- **Pure monospace** — JetBrains Mono, Berkeley Mono (terminal/developer)
- **Heavy display grotesk + neutral body** — Druk + Inter, Space Grotesk + DM Sans
- **Humanist warm sans** — DM Sans, Figtree, Plus Jakarta Sans

### 3. Color temperature
The emotional palette direction.
- **Cool dark** — near-black base, cool accent (magenta, cyan, violet)
- **Warm light** — cream/bone base, warm accent (terracotta, coral, amber)
- **Neutral monochrome** — pure black + pure white + single spot color
- **Vibrant/saturated** — multiple strong colors, gradient accents
- **Pastel/soft** — muted tones, low saturation, gentle
- **Iridescent/chromatic** — gradient that shifts through multiple hues

### 4. Component shape language
How corners, borders, and surfaces are treated.
- **Sharp** — 0–4px radius, precise, architectural
- **Medium** — 8–12px radius, balanced, professional
- **Generous** — 16–24px radius, friendly, approachable
- **Fully rounded** — pills, circles, 9999px radius — playful/spatial
- **Hard borders** — 2px+ solid borders, neo-brutalist
- **Glass/translucent** — backdrop-blur, semi-transparent surfaces

### 5. Motion character
How things move and transition.
- **Snappy** — 150ms, crisp ease-out, feels like a precision tool
- **Confident** — 250ms, smooth ease-out, professional and measured
- **Gentle** — 400–600ms, soft ease, unhurried, magazine-like
- **Spring-based** — overshoot bounce, playful, physics-simulated
- **Minimal** — near-instant, almost no animation, terminal-like
- **Atmospheric** — slow, parallax, floating, dreamy

### 6. Information density
How much content per viewport.
- **Spacious editorial** — lots of whitespace, few elements per screen
- **Balanced app** — moderate density, clear hierarchy
- **Compact dashboard** — many widgets, small text, data-rich
- **Dense terminal** — maximum information, minimal chrome

### 7. Emotional register
The voice/personality of the design.
- **Premium editorial** — curator, tastemaker, design magazine
- **Warm craft** — handmade, human, Aesop/Kinfolk
- **Technical futurist** — spatial computing, visionOS, crystalline
- **Playful startup** — energetic, colorful, ambitious
- **Corporate trust** — clean, blue, reliable, professional
- **Indie builder** — raw, honest, thick borders, neo-brutalist

## Common direction archetypes (ready to use)

| Archetype | Layout | Type | Color | Shape | Motion | Density | Emotion |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Dark Editorial** | Card grid | Serif+sans | Cool dark | Medium | Confident | Spacious | Premium |
| **Warm Light** | Card grid | Serif+sans | Warm light | Generous | Gentle | Spacious | Craft |
| **Spatial Glass** | Float/glass | Single sans | Iridescent | Fully rounded | Atmospheric | Balanced | Futurist |
| **App Layout** | Split-pane | Sans+serif voice | Neutral warm | Medium | Snappy | Balanced app | Functional |
| **Terminal Index** | List rows | Monospace | Monochrome | Sharp | Minimal | Dense | Indie builder |
| **Bento Mosaic** | Bento grid | Geometric sans | Vibrant | Generous | Spring | Balanced | Playful startup |
| **Magazine Spread** | Asymmetric | Heavy display | Spot color | Sharp | Confident | Spacious | Premium |
| **Neo-brutalist** | Asymmetric | Grotesk mono | Primary colors | Hard borders | Snappy | Compact | Indie builder |

When proposing alternatives, pick from different rows — never two from the same archetype.
